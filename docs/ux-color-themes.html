Design Principles

Encouraging

Vibrant blues and greens create an optimistic, supportive atmosphere. Success states are celebrated visually.

Age-Appropriate

Professional enough for 9th graders to feel respected, playful enough to stay engaging. Not childish.

Celebratory

Gold and warm tones for achievements. Designed to make streaks and progress feel special and rewarding.

Accessible

WCAG 2.1 AA compliant contrast ratios. Clear visual hierarchy with distinct states for all interactive elements.
